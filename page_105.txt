tv akṛtābhyāgamaḥ kṛtasya nāśaśca /
tathā smṛteḥ pratyabhijñānasya cābhāvaḥ prasajyate /
sa:nkliṣṭvyavadānatvagrāha iti sa:nkleśavyavadānāśrayatvāt /
yogitvagrāha iti ātmani manaso dhāraṇaṃ yogaḥ prāṇamanasor vā nigrahaḥ 
/
ātmano manasāṃ saṃyogaviśeṣo yogaḥ so'syāstīti yogī /
amuktamuktatvagrāhaśceti ātamno bandhād vimuktir iti yo vikalpaḥ //

katham idaṃ daśavidhaṃ kauśalyatattvaṃ mūlatattve'ntarbhavatīti /
kauśalyaṃ hi skandhādiṣu vaicakṣaṇyaṃ /
tat kathaṃ triṣu svabhāveṣv antarbhavati ?
asambhāvayataḥ praśnaḥ /
yatas triṣu svabhāveṣu te skandhādayo'ntarbhūtā iti kauśalyaviṣayā ete 
kauśalyaśabdenodbhāvitāḥ /
na tu tasminn eva kauśalya iti /
ataḥ akuśalyatattvasyāpy adhikāratas tatrāntarbhāvo na tu svarūpata iti 
veditavyaṃ tadyathā phalahetutattvamārgasatyaṃ triṣvantarbhūtam uktam iti 
/
nanvevam api naiva vijñāyante yathā'ntarbhūtā iti pṛcchati katham 
antarbhūtā iti ataścāha -
	parikalpavikalpārthadharmatā'rthena teṣu te /
iti /
teṣu triṣu svabhāveṣu te skandhādayo'ntarbhūtā ityadhikṛtaṃ /
tatra dhātvādibhyaḥ pūrvaṃ skandhānām antarbhūtatvaṃ prakāśayitavyaṃ 
prathamaṃ nirdiṣṭatvāt tatrāpi rūpasyeti /
