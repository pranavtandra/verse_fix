kāyavedanācittadharmasmṛtyupasthānāni catvāri /
teṣām punar vastu kāyavedanācittadharmāḥ tad viṣayatvāt /
tatrāśrayam abhiniveśavastu kāyaḥ /
ātmaparibhogābhiniveśavastu vedanā /
ātmābhiniveśavastu cittaṃ /
ātmasa:nkleśavyavadānābhiniveśavastu dharma iti /
kāyādiṣu yaḥ svasāmānyalakṣaṇā'navabodhaḥ sa tatrā'kauśalaṃ /
tatra svalakṣaṇam anekāśucidravyeṇa sa:ngṛhītātmakatvaṃ kāyasya /
sukhādisvabhāvo vedanāyāḥ /
viṣayaprativijñaptir vijñānasya/
dharmāṇāṃ yathāsambhavaṃ /
sāmānyalakṣanampunarnityadukhaśūnya'nātmādyākārāḥ/

śucisukhanityātmākāreṇa vā vikalpo vastv akauśalam yasmād ajñānaṃ 
darśaṇaṃ vā smṛtyupasthāneṣvāvaraṇaṃ /
prajñāsmṛtisvābhāvye sati smṛtyupasthānānāṃ kasmād vastvakauśalam 
evāvaraṇam ucyate na tu vastvasmaraṇam apīti ?
prajñādhīnapravṛttitvāt /
tathā hi darśanātmakatvāt prajñāyāḥ prajñāpṛṣṭhalabdha ālambane 
smṛtyādayaḥ pravartante na tu svātantryeṇa prajñāvat /
tasmān nā'smaraṇamatrāvaraṇam uktam iti /
sampyakprahāṇeṣu kauṣīidyaṃ /

vīryaṃ hy atrotpannā'nutpannavipakṣaprahāṇā'nutpātaye 
utpannapratipakṣasya bhūyobhāvotpādāya ca pravartamānaṃ 
samyakprahāṇākhyāṃ pratilabhate /
tat punar bhedābhāve'pi phalabhedāc caturdhā vyavasthāpyata iti smṛtiḥ 
/
ataḥ samyak prahāṇānāṃ vīryātmakatvād ālasyam āvaraṇam uktaṃ /
ṛddhipādeṣu samādher dvayahīnatāvaraṇam iti /
anenaddharyata ity ṛddhiḥ /
sā ca cchandavīryacittamīmāmsāsamādhiḥ /
saivardhhipādaḥ ākāśagamananirmāṇādikāyā ṛddheḥ pratiṣṭhābhūtatvāt 
/
gamanaṃ vā ṛddhiḥ ākāśagamanādikā samṛddhir ityarthaḥ /
tasyāḥ pādā ṛddhipādāḥ tadādhāratvāt /
saiva cchandasamādhyādicaturvidhā samādhiḥ /
tatracchandasamādhau chandaprādhānyaṃ tad dvāreṇa samādhilābhāt /
na tu vīryādayas tatra na santi chandasahāyatvād apradhānās tu te 
tatreti na te prakāśitāḥ /
evaṃ vīryacittamīmāṃsā samādhiṣu vācyaṃ /
samādher dvayahīnatāvaraṇam uktaṃ /
na taj jñāyate ata āha - aparipūryā ca cchandavīryacittamīmāṃsānām 
anyatamavaikalyād bhāvanayā ca prahāṇasaṃskāravaikalyād iti /
tatra paripūrihīnatā tāsāṃ chandavīryacittamīmāṃsānām 
anyatamavaikalyāt tadādhipatyasya samādher abhāvād bhāvanā hīyata 
ity aṣṭaprahāṇasaṃskārāṇām anyatama-vaikalyaṃ /
te punaḥ śraddhācchandavīryapraśrabdhismṛtisamprajanyacetanopekṣātmakāḥ
