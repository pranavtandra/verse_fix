āsvavasthāsu dharmadhātor aprahīṇaniravaśeṣamalatvād aśuddhāvasthety 
ucyate /
aśuddhaśuddhāvasthā śaikṣāṇām iti /
atra ca 
phalasakaraṇīyaviśeṣottarapraveśaniryāṇavyākaraṇakathikābhiṣekāvasthā 
gṛhyante /

etāsvapyavasthāsu dharmādhātoḥ prahīṇāprahīṇakleśatām 
upādāyāśuddhaśuddhāvasthety ucyate /
viśuddhāvasthā'śaikṣāṇām 
ityakaraṇīyaviśeṣānuttaraprāptyanuśaṃsākṛtyānuṣṭhānāvasthā 
viśuddhāvasthetyucyate aśaikṣāṇāṃ niravaśeṣakleśaprahāṇāt /
etāścāvasthāḥ pṛthagjanaśaikṣāṇāṃ vyavasthāpyante /
teṣāṃ ca bahudhā vyavasthānaṃ kathaṃ vijñāpanīyam ityata āha -
	pudgalāṇāṃ vyavasthānaṃ yathāyogamato mataṃ /
iti /
yathāyogam iti yathāsambhavaṃ /
ata ityato'vasthāprabhedād ityarthaḥ /
ayaṃ gotrastho'yam avatīrṇa ityevam ādi /
āditagrahaṇād ayaṃ prayuktaḥ - srotāapannaḥ sakṛdāgāmī anāgāmī 
arhan sāla:nkāraḥ bodhisattvaḥ tathāgataḥ adhimukticārī 
śuddhyādhyāśayacārī niryāyī vyākṛtaḥ dhārmikathikaḥ abhiṣiktaḥ 
bodhiprāptaḥ bhogī parārthakṛd iti /
