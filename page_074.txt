dānādiṣu pravartanaṃ tat pratipakṣeṇa mātsaryādinā'nabhibhavāt /
kimatrāvaraṇaṃ ?
apratisaṃkhyānabhāvanā daurbalyaṃ /
ātmaparayor dharmasambhogaparipācanāvaraṇam iti /
sambhogikāparṣanmaṇḍale vyavasthānaṃ gambhīrodāramahāyānadharmasya 
sambhogo vicitrākārair nirmāṇakarmābhidharmadeśanayā sattvaparipākaś 
ca phalaṃ /
tadāvaraṇam punar yathābhūtaśrutārthavicāraṇā mandaprajñatā 
cāvaraṇam iti /
atra ca dravyataḥ ṣaṭ pāramitāḥ nāmato daśa /
prajñāpāramitāprabhāvitatvāc catasṛṇām pāramitānāṃ /
tatra prajñāpāramitā lokottaranirvikalpajñānaṃ /
tena jñānena krameṇa sarvāvaraṇaprahāṇaṃ /
upāyapraṇidhānabalajñānapāramitāḥ punar 
lokottaramārgapṛṣṭhalabdhajñānasa:ngṛhītāḥ /
jñānapāramitā prakṛṣṭā satī kathaṃ na nirvikalpa iti ?
jñānapāramitā hi nirvikalpajñānapṛṣṭalabdhajñānaṃ /
taj jñānāvabuddhatvaṃ paricchidya svayaṃ ca dharmasambhogaṃ 
pratyanubhavati parāṃś ca pratipācayati /
tac caitan nirvikalpe dvayam api nāstītyato na nirvikalpā 
jñānapāramiteti /
uktaṃ pāramitānām āvaraṇaṃ //2-13//
