tasmād vācyavācakābhiniveśo grāhyagrāhakābhiniveśavad asadarthaṃ 
eveti pratīyate /
evaṃ triṣu svabhāveṣu skandhādīnām anantarbhāvād daśavidhaṃ 
kauśalyatattvaṃ mūlatattva eva draṣṭavyaṃ /
skandhādikauśalyam idam idamātmadarśanapratipakṣatvenoktaṃ /
yasmistvarthe skandhādikauśalyaṃ vipakṣātmadṛṣṭapratipakṣaḥ 
skandhādyartho nokta ityataḥ sa idānīm ucyate //3-16//

	{ skandhārthaḥ }
	amekartvābhisa:nkṣepaparicchedārtha āditaḥ /
ādita ityādāv ucyata iti sambadhyate /
āditas tāvat skandhā ucyante /
te ca trividhenārthena veditavyāḥ /
anekatvārthena yat kiñcid rūpam atītānāgatapratyutpannamādhyātmikaṃ 
vā bāhyaṃ vā audārikaṃ vā sūkṣmaṃ vā hīnaṃ vā praṇītaṃ vā yadvā 
dūre yadvā'ntike ityevam anekārthena bahūnām atītānāgatādīnāṃ 
dravyāṇāṃ skandhaśabdenoktatvāt /
abhisa:nkṣipyetivacanād abhisaṃkṣepārthena skandha iti /
abhisaṃkṣiptetyekīkṛtya /
paricchedārthena ca skandha iti sambadhyate /
caśabdaḥ samuccayārthaḥ anekārthābhisaṃkṣepārthadvayasyāpi /
atra kāraṇam āha - skandhānāṃ rūpādilakṣaṇasya vedanādilakṣaṇebhyaḥ 
pṛthag vyavasthāpanād iti /
pṛthakparicchinna iti paryāyaḥ /
kim atra kāraṇam ebhis tribhira:ngaiḥ skandhārtho vyavasthāpyate ?
rāśyartho hi skandhārtha ityata evaṃ vyavasthāpyate /
evañca loke rāśyartho dṛṣṭaḥ /
yo'neko'bhisaṃkṣītaḥ paricchinnaḥ sa rāśir ityucyate /
yathā rūpaskandha evaṃ vedanādayo'pi skandhā veditavyāḥ /
tatra yo'yam ekātmagrāho yathā puruṣa evedaṃ sarvaṃ yad bhūtaṃ yac ca 
bhāvyam ityādi tasya
