bhogasa:ngṛhītatamedevottaram uktaṃ /
tad dvayaṃ śeṣāṇi vijñānānyadhipativastutvena bhuñjata iti bhogaḥ /
athavā annapānādiko'rtho bhujyata iti bhogaḥ /
tan nirbhāsaṃ vijñānaṃ bhogasa:ngṛhītaṃ /
etat saprajñapti sahetukaṃ nimittaṃ sasamprayogaṃ 
paratantrasvabhāvasa:ngṛhītatvāt saṃskṛtaṃ veditavyaṃ /
samprayogaścaittāḥ /
tat saṃskṛtaṃ na kevalaṃ tadeveti darśayati /
kasya punas tannimittaṃ ?
nāmakāyādīnāṃ /
teṣāṃ tat svabhāvaviśeṣasaṃsūcakatvāt tadanusāreṇa ca 
svabhāvaviśeṣābhiniveśināṃ cittacaittānāṃ manaudgrahavikalpākhyānāṃ 
nimittaṃ /
manaudgrahavikalpaścālayavijñānasya nimittaṃ /
tasmin vāsanapoṣaṇatvād ityeke /
anye tu manyante - svabhāvaviśeṣaprajñāpanāt prajñapter nimittaṃ 
saṃjñāṃ nimittodgrahaṇātmiketi vacanāt /
pratiṣṭhādehabhogasaṃgṛhītam ālayavijñānaṃ nidānanimittaṃ 
vāsanāpoṣaṇatvāt /
ālambananimittañca mana udgrahavikalpā ālambananimittīkaraṇārthena /
ālambanālambana nimittaṃ cobhayathā nimittīkaraṇād iti /
tatra mano yan nityaṃ manyanākāram iti /
ahaṃ mameti yan nityaṃ manyate tanmata ucyate /
tac ca samprayuktam ātmamohātmadṛṣṭyātmasnehātmamānaiḥ pañcabhiś ca 
sarvatragair vedanāsaṃjñācetanāsparśamanaskārair iti /
udgrahaḥ pañca vijñānakāyā iti viṣayasvarūpagrahaṇalakṣaṇatvāt 
svasaṃvedyā ityākhyātum aśakyatvād udgraha ucyate /
vikalpo manovijñānaṃ /
tasya dharmasvasāmānyalakṣaṇavikalpakatvād iti /
etatsarvaṃ saprajñaptisahetukaṃ nimittaṃ karmakleśābhyām 
abhisaṃskṛtatvāt saṃskṛtaṃ /
asaṃskṛtaṃ punaḥ praśamaś ca nirodhaḥ praśamārthaś ca tathateti yuktaṃ 
nirodhasya praśamasvarūpatvaṃ sannimittaduḥkhapraśamalakṣaṇāt /
kathaṃ tathatā praśamārtham ucyata iti ?
praśamasyārtha iti kṛtvā /
nanu ca nirodhena tathatayā na kaścit sambandho'sti kathaṃ praśamārthatvaṃ 
?
tathatāyā mārgālambanatvāt /
mārgasya punaḥ kathaṃ praśamatvaṃ ?
tena praśamanāt /
nirvāṇaṃ praśamaḥ praśamanāt /
anena ca praśamanāt praśamo mārgaḥ /
evaṃ vigrahabhedād ekābhidhānena nirvāṇamārgayor grahaṇaṃ praśama iti /
mārgasatyaṃ punaḥ kiṃ saṃskṛtam asaṃskṛtaṃ ?
saṃskṛtaṃ utpādyatvāt /
yadi karmakleśābhyām anabhisaṃskṛtād asaṃskṛtena ca prabhāvitād 
asaṃskṛtam iti brūyān na doṣaḥ syād iti /
ityetenārthena skandhādiṣu jñānaṃ skandhādikauśalyaṃ veditavyam iti /
