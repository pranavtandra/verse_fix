anudharmapratipattyantaram antadvayavarjane pratipattir vaktavyetyataḥ 
pṛcchati - antadvayavarjane pratipattiḥ katametyata āha - yā ratnakūṭe 
nāmadharmaparyāyeṇa samyak pratipatteḥ svabhāvapratipādanārthaṃ 
madhyamā pratipadupadiṣṭā /
tadyathā - kāśyapa iha mahāratnakūṭe dharmaparyāye śikṣitukāmena 
bodhisattvena yoniśodharmaprayuktena bhavitavyaṃ /
katamaśca yoniśodharmaprayogaḥ ?
yaduta - sarvadharmāṇāṃ bhūtapratyavekṣā /
yā nātmapratyavekṣā na 
sattvanajīvanapoṣanapudgalanamanujanamānavapratyavekṣā /
iyam ucyate kāśyapa madhyamā
