vyavacchedārtham āha - tat pratipakṣa iti /
anye tv anutpattimatvād asaṃskṛtam aṣṭavidham apyatra manyante /
pratipakṣastvanāsravā cetanā punar bhāvanabhisaṃskaraṇād iti /
tan nirodho grāhyo grāhako vetyantaḥ /
tac chabdena vijñānādayo jarāmaraṇaparyavasānāḥ sambadhyante /
nirodho na vijñāyate kiṃ bhāvaḥ kāraṇaṃ vetyāha - yena tan nāmarūpaṃ 
nirudhyata iti /
kena punas tan nāmarūpaṃ nirudhyate /
nāmarūpapratipakṣeṇā'nāsraveṇa mārgeṇa /
vidyā cā'vidyā cādvayam etad advaidhīkāram ityetad evātra jñānaṃ 
saparivāraṃ mārgaḥ /
ataś ca nirudhyate'neneti nirodhaḥ /
kaḥ punar atrāntaḥ /
ayam eva grāhyagrāhakābhiniveśaḥ /
kiṃ kāraṇaṃ ?
advayaprakṛtiṣv avidyādiṣv avidyamānadvayādhyāropapravṛttavāt /
evaṃ grāhyagrāhakānto dvidhā - kṛṣṇaśuklapakṣabhedeneti /
tatra kṛṣṇapakṣe dvādaśabhavā:ngāni avidyā yāvaj jarāmaraṇaṃ /
evaṃ śuklapakṣe'pyvidhyādinirodhabhedena dvādaśaprakāra 
evāvidhyānirodho yāvaj jarāmaraṇanirodha iti /
atra ca nirydhyante'enā'vidyādaya iti tat pratipakṣabhūto mārga eva 
nirodhaśabdenoktaḥ /
tat parivarjanārtham iti grāhyagrāhakāntaparivarjanārthaṃ 
madhyamāpratipad uktyabhisambadhyate /
sā ca na vijñāyata ityata āha - vidyā cā'vidyā cādvayam etad iti /
kathaṃ vidyā cāvidyā ca yāvaj jarāmaraṇaṃ tan nirodhaścā'dvayam ityata 
āha - vidyāvidyādīnāṃ grāhyagrāhakatvābhāvād iti /
tad abhāvastv avidyādīnām advayaprakṛtitvāt /
athavā vidyā'vidyeti parikalpitasvabhāvo'taś ca parikalpitasvabhāvayor 
vidyā'vidyayorabhāvasamatayā vidyā cā'vidyā cādvayam iti /
evaṃ saṃskārāṇām asaṃskṛtasya ca vijñānasya tan nirodhasya ca yāvaj 
jarāmaraṇasya tannirodhasya ceti vaktavyaḥ /
kā punar atra madhyamā pratipat /
etad eva jñānaṃ vidyā cāvidyā cādvayam etadavaidhīkāram iti na 
vidyotpādayitavyā nā'vidyā nirodhayitavyā /
na saṃskārādayaḥ praheyāḥ /
na tannirodhaḥ prāptavya iti yadevaṃ jñānam etadavaidhīkāraṃ //
