iti /
muktiḥ saṃkleśaprahāṇaṃ /
tat saṃkleśaprahāṇāñca mārgabhāvanātaḥ /
tatra yadi dharmāṇāṃ śūnyatā āgantukair upakleśair anutpanne'pi 
pratipakṣe apiśabdād utpanna iva na saṃkliṣṭā bhaved evaṃ sati 
saṃkleśābhāvād ayatnata eva muktāḥ sarvasattvā bhaveyuḥ /
atyatnata eveti pratipakṣam antareṇaiva /
na tu pratipakṣam antareṇa prāṇinām asti mokṣa ity avaśyaṃ pṛthag 
janāvasthāyāṃ tathātāyām āgantukair malaiḥ 
saṃkliṣṭatā'bhyupagantavyeti /
evaṃ śūnyatāyāḥ saṃkliṣṭaḥ prabhedaḥ sādhito bhavati /
idānīṃ viśuddhiprabhedaṃ sādhayann āha -
	viśuddhā ced bhavennā'sau vyāyāmo niṣphalo bhavet /
iti /
dehinām iti sambadhyate /
athotpanne'pi pratipakṣe apiśabdhād anutpanna iva na viśuddhā bhaved evaṃ 
sati mokṣārtha ārambho niṣphalo bhavet pratipakṣabhāvanayāpi 
tanmalasyā'vigamāt samalasya vimokṣānupapatteś ca /
na tu mokṣārtha ārambho niṣpahal iṣyate /
tasmād avaśyaṃ pratipakṣābhyāsād āgantukopakleśavigamāc chūnyatāyā 
viśuddhirabhyupagantavyeti /
evaṃ śūnyatāyā viśūddhiprabhedaḥ sādhito bhavati /
atra saṃkleśadharmopādānāt saṃkleśo viśuddhi dharmagrahaṇād viśuddhiḥ /
na tu śūnyatāyā sākṣāt saṃkleśo viśuddhir veṣyate, 
dharmaparatantratvādharmatāyāḥ /
ata evāḥ
	muktāḥ syuḥ sarvadehinaḥ /
iti /
ata hi dehina iti tadupādānamevoktaṃ /
anyathā vā yadi śūnyatāyāḥ sākṣāt samkleṣo viṣuddadirvā	 bhavet 
tataḥ kaḥ sambandho dehibhir bhavedyena śūnyatāviśuddhiś ca dehināṃ
