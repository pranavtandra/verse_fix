ityatrerṣyāmātsarye na gṛhyetām, tayorupakleśatvāt /
jñeya evāvaraṇam iti jñeyāvaraṇam /
tena jñeyaprāvṛtatvāj jñānasya viṣaya eva nāsti /
jñāne vā jñeyāvaraṇaṃ, jñeyasya jñānotpattipratibandhakatvād iti 
jñeyāvaraṇamityatra madhyamapadalopo draṣṭavyaḥ /
tailapātram iva /
tatpunarakliṣṭamajñātam /
bodhisattvagotrakāṇāṃ asvaparārthasampatprakarṣalābhāya 
buddhatvaprāptaye puṇyajñānasambhāraṃ sarvathā sarvatrā'nutiṣṭhatāṃ 
tad dvayam api pratibandhakabhūtam /
ityatastadāvaraṇaṃ vyavasthāpyate bodhisattvagotrakāṇām /
tatra vyāpi sarvaviṣayaṃ sakalaṃ sarvathā vyāpnoti /
kathaṃ sakalaṃ vyāpnotīti jñāyata iti cet - sākalyādāvaraṇaṃ 
vyāpītyucyate iti hetunirdeśāt /
atha vā vyāpiśabdo niravaśeṣārtha iti pradarśanāya sākalyādivacanam /
prādeśikaṃ kleśāvaraṇaṃ śrāvakādigotrakāṇām iti 
svārthamātrāvaraṇādupacārādavyāpitvāc ca prādeśikākhyā /
kiṃ śrāvakapratyekabuddhānām akliṣṭamajñānaṃ nāsti yasmāt teṣāṃ 
kleśāvaraṇam eva vyavasthāpyata iti?
bhavatu, na tu tadāvaraṇaṃ teṣām, tasmin vidyamāne 
śrāvakapratyekabuddhabodhiprāpteḥ /
nanvajñānaṃ duḥkhādisatyaviṣaye jñānotpattipratibaṃdhakatvam eva 
śrāvakādīnām /
tatas teṣām api dvayāvaraṇaṃ bhavet /
tasmān na vaktavyaṃ kleśāvaraṇaṃ śrāvakādigotrakāṇām iti /
naitadevam, ajñānaṃ hi kleṣṭaṃ jñānapratipakṣabhūtaṃ saṃsāramūlaṃ ca 
kleśṣāvaraṇameveti vyavasthāpyate, na tu jñeyāvaraṇaṃ, saṃśaya iva /
yathā vimatir hi duḥkhādisatyaviṣaye vicikitsā kleśaścocyate na tu 
viṣayāntare /
viṣayāntare hi saṃśaya eva, na kleśo na vicikitsā /
evavṃ duḥkhasatyādiviṣaye evā'jñānamavidyā kleśaścocyate, na tu 
viṣayāntare /
viṣayāntare tvajñānam eva, na cā'vidyā naiva kleśaḥ /
tasmāt tasya jñeyajñānamātrapravṛttāvāvaraṇatvāt tajñeyāvaraṇam 
ucyate na hi kleśakarmajanmanāmapi, tāni cotpadyanta evāvidyāyā eva /
yadyevaṃ bodhisattvānām āvaraṇaṃ na vyavasthāpyate /
naitadevam, bodhisattvānāṃ hi śrutasambhāraprabhāvitatvāt 
teṣāmāvaraṇaṃ jñeyāvaraṇam /
taduktam -
	āryho'pyagyūḥ pañca vidyāpadāni
	nārabhya syāt kadāpi sarvajñaḥ /
	tato'nyeṣāṃ nigrahā'nugrahārthaṃ
	svajñānārthaṃ vā'rabhet tatra yatnam //
iti /
udṛktaṃ tu mṛdvapi rāgādivastu teṣām eva bodhisattvagotrakāṇāṃ 
rāvakādigotrakāṇañcā'dhimātramabhīkṣṇaṃ ca pravartitaṃ 
rāgādicaritānām āvaranam iti /
yeṣāṃ
