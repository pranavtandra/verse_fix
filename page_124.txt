taddhi kleśāvaraṇaviśuddhijñānagocarād anyasyābhidyotaka āgamaḥ /
tat punar mahāyānaṃ /
sarvākārajñeyapraveśadṛśyatattvaṃ saṃgrahatattvaṃ /
tac ca pañca vastūni tatra sarvajñeyānupraveśāt /
avitathatā'bhidyotanadṛśyatattvaṃ prabhedatattvaṃ /
tena hi bhāvānāmavitathatathatā'nanyatathatayor abhidyotanāt /
ātmagrāhavastusarvābhisandhipraveśadṛśyatattvañceti kauśalayatattvaṃ /
tena hy ātmagrāhavastu sarvābhiprāyamukhena praviśatīti yathā pūrvam 
uktaṃ daśabhir abhiprāyaiḥ skandhādiṣv ātmagrāhapravṛttitvaṃ /
tattvapiṇḍārthavacanaṃ sukhadhāraṇārtham arthād grantham ākarṣatīti /
asammohāparikhedārthañca paridṛṣṭādhikāraparyantasya hi na parikheda 
upajñāyate nāpi sammoho yathoddeśanirdeśādhigamād utapdyate /
uktaṃ tattvaṃ //
