vartamānajanmany upalabdham eva svapne pratibhāsate /
na hy atītānāgatavartamānānubhave suptasya jāgrataś ca kaścid 
viśeṣo'sti /
tasmāt parikalpamātram evaitad anubhūtārthaviṣayaṃ svapne vijñānam iti 
/  atītasya cābhāvān nirviṣayaṃ vijñānam eva svapne'rthābhāsam 
utpadyata iti niścayaḥ /
evaṃ vijñaptimātratvenopalambhād arthā'nupalabdhir bhāvyeti /
ya
arthā'nupalabdhiṃ niścitya vijṇaptimātrasyāpyanupalabdhirjāyata iti/
yatha na vijṇyanād bahiḥ parikalpitaṃ grāhyamastīti  
vijñaptimātratābalena manogrāhyābhāvaṃ praviśati tathā 
grāhyabhāvabalena vijñaptimātrasyāpy abhāvaṃ pratipadyate /
na grāhyābhāve grāhakatvaṃ yujyate /
grāhyam apekṣya tad grāhakasya vyavasthāpanāt /
evam asallakṣaṇaṃ grāhyagrāhakayoḥ parikalpitarūpayoḥ praviśati 
nābhūtaparikalpasyeti darśanaṃ bhavati /
kim arthaṃ punaḥ prathamata eva vijñaptimātrasyaivābhāvaṃ na 
vibhāvayati ?
grāhyapratibaddhatvāddhi grāhakasyopalabhyārthābhāve sukhaṃ praveśaḥ 
syād ālambanasvabhāvavināśāt /
anyathā bhāvāpavādam eva kuryāt /
grāhyagrāhakayoḥ parasparanirapekṣatvāt /
imāñca grāhyagrāhakavikalpātītāṃ jñānabhūmiṃ saṃbhāreṣu 
nirantaraṃ pravartamānaḥ prathamakalpāsaṃkhyeyaparisamāptau praviṣati /
tathā hi vijñaptimātram idaṃ niśritya rūpānupalambhānukūlo 
lokottaramārge prathama uṣmagatākhyaḥ saparivāraḥ samādhirāmukhī 
bhavati /
tasyānantaraṃ mūrdhākhyaḥ /
tasyānantaram aśeṣagrāhyānupalambhād grāhakānupalabhānukūlaḥ 
kṣāntyākhyaḥ samādhiḥ /
tasyānantaram arthānupalabdhiṃ niśritya vijñaptimātrasyāpy anupalabdhiṃ 
bhāvayan laukikāgryadharmākhyaḥ prajñādisahitaḥ saparivāraḥ samādhir 
āmukhībhavati /
tad anantaraṃ darśanamārgaḥ /
tatraiva ca sarvatragadharmadhātvadhigamāt prathamāṃ bhūmiṃ praviśatīti 
/
ayaṃ ca tattvamanaskāro nāvimuktimanaskāro'pramāṇādivat //7//
