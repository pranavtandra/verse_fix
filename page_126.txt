nirvāṇārthaṃ trāsaḥ /
sa ca guṇadoṣānavabodhaḥ sukhādyabhiniveśaś ca satyadarśanād vinivartata 
ityataḥ saṃsārād udvajanārthaṃ nirvāṇe cābhimukhīkaraṇārthaṃ 
catuḥsatyāvatārāya smṛtyupasthānabhāvanaivādāv uktā /
kāyena hi dauṣṭhulyaṃ prabhāvyata iti /
dauṣṭhulyaṃ duḥsthitatā /
tathā hi pratidinam abhisaṃskriyamāṇo vikriyata eva kāya iti kāyena 
dauṣṭhulyaṃ prabhāvyate /
tataś ca kāyaparīkṣayā duḥkhasatyam avatarati /
kim artham etad ityāha - tasya sadauṣṭhulyasaṃskāralakṣaṇatvād iti /
yasmād duḥkhasatyasya sadauṣṭhulyāḥ saṃskārāḥ lakṣaṇaṃ svabhāvaḥ 
kāyena ca dauṣṭhulyaṃ prabhāvyate tasmāt kāyaparīkṣayā duḥkhasatyam 
avatarati /
dauṣṭhulyaṃ na vijñāyata ityata āha - dauṣṭhulyaṃ hi saṃskāraduḥkhateti 
/
saṃskāro bhāvanām utpattiḥ /
sa ca sarvaviplavastv iti dauṣṭhulyamāryāṇāṃ pratikūlatvāc ca duḥkhatā 
/
yathoktaṃ -
	anartho janmedaṃ sati hi janane santi ripavo
		jarā-mṛtyu-vyādhi-vyasana-bandha-prabhṛtayaḥ /
	anarthās te na syur yadi na bhavato nāśavibhavā-
		vajāte vṛkṣe syān na hi dahanadavavāyucyutir api //
tayeti saṃskāraduḥkhatayā na vipariṇāmaduḥkhatāduḥkhaduḥkhatābhyāṃ 
tayor āvyāpitvāt saṃskāraduḥKhatānimittatvāc ca /
sarvaṃ sāsravaṃ vastvāryā duḥkhatā paśyanti nā'nāsavaṃ mārgasatyasya 
janmotpattipratipakṣatvāt na tu pṛthagjanā viparyāsasthānāśrayatvāt /
sūtre'py uktaṃ - samāsataḥ pañcopādānaskandhā duḥkham iti /
saṃskāraduḥkhatā'karmaṇyatetyanye /
na dauṣṭhulyam akarmaṇyātetyapare /
kiṃ kāraṇaṃ ?
akarmaṇyātāyāḥ sāsravatvād avaśyaṃ sāsravatāṃ 
bījakāraṇotpannā'bhyupagantavyā /
sā tv abījāpyarthasantāne kadācid āhārādyapacārād bhavati /
na cāsau dauṣṭhulyaṃ bhavitum arhatya nāsravatvād iti /
ataś ca sāsravadharmabījam ālayavijñānasanniviṣṭaṃ 
dauṣṭhulyenātreṣyate /
ayaṃ sukhaduḥkhā'sukhā'duḥkhasahitaḥ kāyastat phalaṃ /
tasmāt sukhādyavakāśasahitena kāyena pratikṣaṇabhāvinā tad dauṣṭhulyaṃ 
prabhāvyate pratīyata ityarthaḥ /
tadyathā khalavilāntargataṃ bījam a:nkureṇeti /
tasmāt kāyaparīkṣayā duḥkhasatyam avatarati /
atra kāraṇam āha -
