ātmeti śabdena pudgalasamāropānta iti skandhadhātvāyatana vyatiriktam 
avyatiriktaṃ vā vandhyāputravad asantam ātmānaṃ dravyataḥ paśyataḥ 
pudgalasyātmano vasturūpasamāropaṇam antaḥ /
nairātmyam ityapavādāntaḥ /
apavādo hi sato'sattva parikalpa ityuktaḥ /
ātmanastv asattvāt kathaṃ nairātmyam ity apavādānta āpadyata ityata 
āha - prajñaptisato'pyapavādāt nairātmyam apavādānta iti sambadhyate 
/
sa punar dharmāṇāṃ santāno yasmin hetuphalasambandhaḥ prajñāyate /
samudayo vā yatra devamanuṣyaprajñaptiḥ /
tat parivarjanārthaṃ madhyamā pratipad ityātmanaḥ 
sadasattādarśanaparivarjanārthaṃ /
tasyāḥ punar yasyā madhyamāyāḥ pratipado nirūpaṇārtham āha - 
yadātmanairātmyayor madhyamam iti /
kiṃ punas tan madhyaṃ /
nirvikalpaṃ jñānaṃ /
grāhyārthavikalpasyāntatvaṃ darśayitvā 
grāhakārthavikalpasyāntatvapradarśanārtham āha - bhūtaṃ cittam ityādi 
/
athavā pudgalasya samāropāpavādāntatvaṃ darśayitvā dharmasya 
samāropāpavādāntadarśanārtham āha - bhūtaṃ cittam ityādi /
yathā pratibhāsate yathā vā'bhilapyate cittaṃ tathaiva bhūtaṃ vidyata 
iti /ayaṃ dharmasamāropāntaḥ abhūtasya cittasvabhāvabhūtatvena 
samāropāt /
abhūtaṃ cittam ityapavādāntaḥ /
tasya hi grāhyagrāhakarahitātmanā nirabhilāpyena ca vidyamānam 
apyabhūtam iti paśyataḥ sarvātmanā dharmasvabhāvāpavādād 
apavādāntaḥ /
tat parivarjanārtham iti samāropāpavādarahitasya dharmanairātmyasya 
pravitedhārthaṃ madhyamā pratipad /
sā yatra nacittaṃ na cetanā na mano na vijñānam iti /
abhiniveśā na pravartanta iti vākyaśeṣaḥ /
yatra vā viṣaye na cittaṃ sāsravavāsanābhiścittātmakaṃ pravartate na 
cetanā'bhisaṃskāralakṣaṇā na mano manyanākāraṃ na vijñānañca 
vikalpanam iyaṃ madhyamā pratipad ityucyate /
tatra cittam ālayavijñānaṃ sarvasrāvadharmavāsanābhiścittatvāt /
cetanā manaskarma kuśalākuśalāvyākṛteṣu cittapreraṇakarmikā /
manaḥ kliṣṭaṃ manaḥ /
vijñānaṃ ṣaḍvijñānakāyāḥ /
akuśalādayo dharmāḥ saṃkleśa iti vipakṣāntaḥ /
kuśalādayo vyavadānam iti
