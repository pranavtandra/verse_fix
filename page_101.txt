nāmnaḥ parikalpiteneti /
yathānāmārthābhāvān nāmnaḥ parikalpitena sa:ngrahaḥ /
evam adhikārasa:ngrahaḥ parikalpitena nāmno na svabhāvasa:ngrahaḥ iti yad 
uktaṃ niṣsvabhāvasiddhatvāt parikalpitaḥ svabhāvo 
nimittādipañcavastubhir na sa:ngṛhīta iti tan na virudhyate tatra 
svabhāvasa:ngrahasya vivakṣitatvāt /
	samyag jñānasatattvasya
iti /
sadā śobhanaṃ vā tattvaṃ satattvaṃ tathatā śūnyatālambanādvayajñānaṃ 
tat pṛṣṭhalabdhaśuddhalaukikaṃ ca samyag jñānaṃ /
tathatāsamyag jñānayor avikārā'viparyāsapariniṣpattyā yathākramaṃ 
pariniṣpannatvād ekenaiva pariniṣpannena svabhāvena sa:ngraha iti /
tad uktaṃ sa:ngrahatattvaṃ //3-13//

	{ prabhedatattvaṃ }
idānīṃ prabhedatattvaṃ katividhaṃ kathañca tanmūlatattve sa:ngṛhītam 
iti na vijñāyata ityataḥ pṛcchati - prabhedatattvaṃ mūlatattve katham 
veditavyam iti /
ata āha - saptavidhaṃ prabhedatattvaṃ /
katham ityata āha - pravṛttitattvaṃ lakṣaṇatattvam iti vistaraḥ /
yaiva ca sandhinirmocaranasūtre saptavidhā tathatā nirdiṣṭā 
pravṛttitathatāyāvat samyag pratipattitathateti saivā'tra saptavidhaṃ 
prabhedatattvam ityuktā pravṛttitattvaṃ yāvat samyakpratipattitattvam iti 
/
tatra pravṛttitattvaṃ yā saṃsārā'navarāgratā saiva ca pravṛtti tathatā 
yasmān na jātu tasyāgratāyogo bhavati /
lakṣaṇatattvaṃ pudgaladharmanairātmyaṃ /
