pāramitānuṣṭhānaṃ /
sā ca pāramitāpratipattir mahāyāna eva nirdiṣṭā nānyatra yāna iti 
mahāyānam evānuttaram iti darśayati /
sā ca pratipattir yiaḥ prakāraiḥ pāramitāsu prayujyamānasyānuttarā 
bhavati tān prakārān darśayann āha -
	pratipattis tu ṣaḍvidhā
iti //

	{ paramā pratipattiḥ }
sā ca pāramitāpratipattir na vijñāyate kathaṃ paramā yāvat katham 
aviśiṣṭetyata āha - tatra paramā dvādaśātmikā //
iti /
dvādaśaprakārā paramā mahatī niruttaretyarthaḥ /
tat prakārapradarśanārtham āha -
	audāryam āyatattvañca
