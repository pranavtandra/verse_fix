na śūnyaṃ nā'pi cā'śūnyaṃ
iti /
kim artham asau śloka ārabdha iti ?
sarvasya saṃskṛtasyāsaṃskṛtasya ca dvayarahitatājñāpanārthaṃ /
evaṃ ca hyekāntapratikṣepeṇa sarvam idaṃ na śūnyaṃ nāśūnyam iti 
prajñāpāramitāpāṭhābhiprāyatāviṣkṛtā bhavati /
anyathā pūrvāparaviruddham etat syāt /
kiñca madhyamāpratipat prakāśanārthaṃ /
anyathaikāntikaśūnyataivāśanyānto vā syāt /
	apavādasamāropapratiṣedhopasaṃhārārthaṃ vā'syārambhaḥ /
hetupratyayapratibaddhātmalābhād abhūtaparikalpaḥ saṃskṛtaṃ /
śūnyatā punas tan nirapekṣatvād asaṃskṛtaṃ /
vidhīyate prajñāpāramitādimadhye paṭhyate /
sattvād abhūtaparikalpasya na saṃskṛtaṃ śūnyam abhūtaparikalpātmatvena 
/
asatvād dvayasya /
grāhyagrāhakātmanā śūnyaṃ /
śūnyātāyās tu sarvam abhūtaparikalpe tad dharmateti kṛtvā /
śūnyatāyām apy abhūtaparikalpo dharmirūpeṇa vidyate /
evam asaṃskṛtam api dharmatārūpeṇa na śūnyaṃ /
abhāvasaṃjñakena dvayena svarūpaśūnyaṃ /
sā ca madhyamā pratipad iti ratnakūṭādimadhye'stīti kāśyapāyam 
eko'ntaḥ /
nāstītyayaṃ dvitīyo'ntaḥ /
yadetayor dvayor antayor madhyamiyam ucyate kāśyapa madhyamā pratipad 
dharmāṇāṃ bhūtapratyavekṣeti sā madhyamā pratipadevam anulomitā /
sarvam iti saṃskṛtam asaṃskṛtañca /
