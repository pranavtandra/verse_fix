lakṣaṇaparicchedaḥ prathamaḥ

	āryamañjuśriye kumārabhūtāya namaḥ /
uttamajano hi prāyaśo guruṃ śraddhādevatāṃ cābhyarcyakarmasu pravartata 
ityayamapyahamuttamajananayamanuvartī madhyāntavibhāgasūtrabhāṣyaṃ 
cikīrṣustatpraṇeturvaktuś ca pūjāṃ kṛtvā tadarthavibhāgāya prayukta 
iti pratipādayannāha -
	śāstrasyā'sya praṇetāram
ityādi /
evaṃ kṛtvā ko guṇaḥ prāpyate ?
guṇavato hitakāriṇaś ca pūjayatāmeṣāṃ puṇyamupacīyate /
puṇya upacite samārambhād vighnavināyakairanupahatānalpena prayāsena 
samāpayantīti /
athavā praṇetṛpraṇeyapravacanāt sūtrapraṇetṛvaktṛvṛttīṣu 
gauravotpādanārthamāha-
	 śāstrasyā'sya praṇetāram
iti sarvam /
tatra praṇetṛvacanāt sūtre gauravam utpadyate /
yasyā'sya kārikāśāstrasyāryamaitreyaḥ praṇetā /
sa caikajātipratibandhāt sarvabodhisattvābhijñādhāraṇīpratisaṃvit 
samādhīndriyakṣāntivimokṣaiḥ paramaṃ pāraṃ gataḥ sarvāsu 
bodhisattvabhūmiṣu
