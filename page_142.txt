prakarṣāvasthānāt /
tac ca śrāvakādibhedena tridhābhidyate /
niyatāniyataprabhedena ṣaḍvidhaṃ bhavati /
avatārāvasthā utpāditabodhicittasyeti /
sā ca śrāvakapratyekabuddhatathāgatagotrāṇāṃ yathākramaṃ 
śrāvakapratyekabuddhasamyaksambuddhabodhiṣu cittotpādāt trividhā bhavati 
/
tasya prayogamūlabhūtatvāt tasminn utpanne prayogāvatāra iti tad 
utpādaḥ prayogāvatārāvasthety ucyate /
prayogāvasthā cittotpādādūrdhvam aprāpte phala iti /
utpāditabodhicittasya dānaśīlādiko 
balabhāvanāparyavasānakuśalasamārambhaḥ prathamaphalaprāpaka upāya iti 
sa prayoga ucyate /
kathaṃ hetvavatārāvasthā bodhipakṣabhāvanāvasthety ucyate ?
taddhetutvāt /
ata evoktaṃ - uttarottaraphalaṃ gotrāc cittotpāda ityevam ādi /
phalāvasthā prāpte prathamaphale /
phalārthaṃ hi prayujyate /
ataḥ prayogānantaraṃ phalāvasthā /
sakaraṇīyāvasthā hi śaikṣasya /
tasya phalānām 
aparisamāptatvāt-phalānantaraprāptipratikaraṇīyasadbhāvāt /
akaraṇīyāvasthā'śaikṣasyeti /
aśaikṣo hi tridhātukavītarāgāt /
tasya phalānāṃ parisamāptatvāt phalāntaraprāptikaraṇīyāsadbhāvād 
akaraṇīyāvasthā /
viśeṣāvasthā'bhijñādiguṇaviśeṣasamanvāgatasyeti /
samāne'rthattve'bhijñādibhir guṇaviśeṣaiḥ samanvāgata itarasmād 
viśiṣṭā'bhijñādiguṇaviśeṣaniṣpannāvasthā viśeṣāvasthetyucyate /
uttarāvasthā śrāvakapratyekabuddhebhyo bhūmipraviṣṭasya bodhisattvasya /
svaparārthakārikasya lokottarasya mārgasyobhayanairātmyaprativedhasya 
lābhena tadanyayānottarabhūtāt tadvasthottarāvasthetyucyate /
anuttarāvasthā buddhasya /
kim arthaṃ ?
ata āha - tata ūrdhvam avasthā'bhāvād iti /
sarvaguṇadoṣaprakarṣaniṣṭhādhiṣṭhānatvād buddhasya tata ūrdhvamanyā 
viśiṣṭāvasthā na vidyata iti saivānuttaretyucyate /
iyaduktaṃ parisamāptāpi niravaśeṣāvasthā bodhisattvānām 
ekādaśabhūmiprabheda-sandarśanārtham 
adhimukticartyādyavasthābhiṣekādhya vastāvasānāḥ punar uktā /
buddhānāṃ tu kāyatrayaprabhedapradarśanārthaṃ prāptyavasthādikāḥ 
punas tisro nirdiṣṭāḥ /
tatra hetvavatārāvasthā bodhisattvetarasādhāraṇā veditavyā /
tatrā'dhimuktyavasthā bodhisattvānāṃ sarvasyām adhimukticaryābhūmāv 
iti /
cittotpādād ūrdhvaṃ yāvan na bhūmipraveśo'trāntare'dṛṣṭatattvo'pi 
dṛṣṭatattvaṃ iva
